Last updated: May 25, 2026

OfferLaunch ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at offerlaunch.ai and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and company details when you submit forms or applications

  • Communications you send to us, including email correspondence
  • Newsletter signup information if you subscribe to updates

We may also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, and referring URLs, through cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and process applications

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze traffic and improve our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at team@offerlaunch.ai.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
